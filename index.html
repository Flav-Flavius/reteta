Rețetă șnițel

Ingredients:

  • 2 pieces of veal (approx. 180-200g each)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 2-3 tablespoons of breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 quarter lemon
  • Oil for frying

Preparation:

  1. Prepare meat: trim tendons and flatten with a meat hammer.
  2. Meat thickness should be halved.
  3. Mix flour with salt and pepper.
  4. Beat the egg with a tablespoon of water.
  5. Coat the meat in flour, removing excess.
  6. Dip the meat into the beaten egg.
  7. Coat with breadcrumbs, pressing gently.
  8. Fry until golden and crispy.
  9. Place on paper towels to absorb oil.
  10. Serve traditionally with potato salad, lemon, and wine.
